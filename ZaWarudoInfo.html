This is DIO, a man of infinite lifetimes and unimaginable power, both with his Stand (Za Warudo) and the ladies. His Stand, which is a physical manifestation of his life energy, has its own abilities. His Stand has extreme speed and precision, much like that of Jotaro's Stand, discussed here, and has the ability to stop time (the length of which is extendable and the limit thereof is indiscernable). He is the main antagonist in "JoJo's Bizarre Adventure Part 3: Stardust Crusaders", the first season in which Stands are introduced as a whole. DIO eventually has to combat each of the Stardust Crusaders; Jotaro Kujo, Joseph Joestar, Polnareff, Avdol (Avdol has been killed off right before DIO's main introduction), and Kakyoin: DIO defeated them one by one until Jotaro successfully counters DIO's time stopping power with a perfectly timed time-stop of his own, just before DIO delivered the final blow in their intense battle.
